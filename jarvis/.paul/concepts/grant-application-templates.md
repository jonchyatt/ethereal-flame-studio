# Grant Application Master Templates

**Purpose:** AI grant automation. Agent Zero + Jarvis select and adapt these templates when filling grant applications across all 5 ventures. Templates are written to be immediately usable with minimal adaptation — the AI replaces bracketed variables with data from `grant-entity-profiles.md`.

**Last updated:** 2026-03-15
**Companion file:** `grant-entity-profiles.md`

---

## AI USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

1. Read the grant application questions carefully
2. Load the correct entity profile from `grant-entity-profiles.md`
3. Match each question to the closest template section below
4. Replace [BRACKETS] with entity-specific data
5. Adjust length to match word count requirements
6. Never exceed the stated word limit by more than 10%
7. If no template fits, flag for human authorship

**Organization Introduction:**

We are the only provider offering all three modalities in an integrated, autism-affirming protocol in Washington County.

**Need Statement:**

Washington County, Utah is home to approximately [N] children diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder, a number that mirrors the national prevalence of 1 in 36 (CDC, 2023). Yet the county has [FEW/ZERO] providers offering non-pharmaceutical therapeutic interventions specifically designed for this population. Families who seek HBOT typically pay $150-300 per session, with most protocols requiring 20-40 sessions. For a family earning Washington County's median household income of [FILL], this is financially impossible. Meanwhile, research continues to accumulate supporting the benefits of these therapies: [CITE 1-2 KEY STUDIES]. The gap between evidence and access is not a research problem — it is an equity problem.

**Program Description:**

[SPA NAME]'s Autism Access Program provides subsidized access to our integrated therapy protocol for low-income families of autistic children and adults in Washington County.
