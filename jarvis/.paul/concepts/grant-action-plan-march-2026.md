## ⚠️ CRITICAL STRATEGY CORRECTION: DSPD / HBOT Medicaid Coverage

**Previous claim:** "DSPD Medicaid waiver reimburses HBOT for autism — Priority #1 for med spa"
**Actual finding:** HBOT for autism is NOT FDA-approved → Medicaid will NOT cover HBOT under autism diagnosis. Utah's Autism Waiver was also terminated.

**What this means for Venture C:**
- HBOT for autism remains **private pay only** (out of pocket for families)
- Grants to subsidize family access become MORE important, not less
- VA pathway still viable: HBOT for TBI/PTSD in veterans IS covered by VA
- EPSDT (children under 21) — may cover some services; contact Utah Medicaid to confirm exact scope
- PBM/photobiomodulation under different CPT codes may still be coverable under some diagnoses
- Call DSPD at (801) 538-4200 to explore what IS enrollable

**Grant implications:** Focus Venture C applications on the "access gap" narrative — autism families CAN'T get this therapy because insurance doesn't cover it, so grant funding makes it possible. This is actually a **stronger grant story** than "we want to expand a reimbursed service."
