## Quality Bar (CRITICAL CONTEXT)

User explicitly wants **superlative, wife-impressive UI**. Every component must feel premium:
- `variant="glass"` for read-only containers, `variant="glass-interactive"` for tappable — NEVER `default`
- Staggered `fadeInUp` entrance animations (400ms ease-out, 50-80ms stagger)
- Hover elevation (scale + shadow + border brighten)
- Spring easing: `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` for transforms
- Glass surfaces: `bg-black/60 backdrop-blur-md border border-white/10`
- Frosted chrome: `bg-zinc-950/80 backdrop-blur-xl` on fixed nav elements
- No flat card lists — grouped sections with summary heroes
