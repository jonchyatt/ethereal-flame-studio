## 1. Title Templates

### SEO Principles for 2026

- **Front-load primary keyword** - Place within first 3-5 words
- **Keep under 60 characters** - Avoid mobile truncation
- **Include duration** - Viewers filter by length for meditation content
- **Use power words** - "Deep," "Calming," "Powerful," "Ultimate"
- **Include year sparingly** - Only when freshness matters

## 4. Category Recommendations

### Primary Category: **People & Blogs** or **Entertainment**

| Category | When to Use | Pros | Cons |
|----------|-------------|------|------|
| **People & Blogs** | Default for meditation | Lower competition, personal feel | Less discoverability |
| **Entertainment** | VR/360 content, visually stunning | High traffic | High competition |
| **Music** | Audio-focused content | Music discovery algorithms | Must own all music rights |
| **Education** | Guided/instructional meditation | Educational audience | Stricter expectations |
| **Film & Animation** | High-production visuals | Creative community | Niche audience |

### Recommended Strategy

1. **Long-form meditation:** People & Blogs
2. **VR/360 experiences:** Entertainment
3. **Shorts:** People & Blogs (consistent with channel)
4. **Music-focused ambient:** Music (only if original compositions)

## 5. Thumbnail Text Overlay Suggestions

### The 6-Word Maximum Rule

Thumbnails should communicate in under 2 seconds. Text should be:
- **Readable at small size** (mobile)
- **High contrast** (white on dark, yellow on dark)
- **Bold sans-serif font** (Impact, Bebas Neue, Montserrat Black)
- **6 words or fewer**

### 5.6 Thumbnail Design Principles

1. **Consistency:** Use same font family across all thumbnails
2. **Brand colors:** Stick to 2-3 colors (warm oranges, deep purples, soft blues)

5. **Avoid clutter:** Single focal point
6. **A/B test:** Use YouTube's native A/B testing for optimization

## 7. End Screen and Card Recommendations

### 7.1 End Screen Strategy (Last 20 Seconds)

**Layout for Meditation Videos:**

```
[                                          ]
[    VIDEO STILL/GENTLE ANIMATION          ]
[                                          ]
[  [SUBSCRIBE]     [BEST FOR VIEWER]       ]
[   Button          Video Element          ]
[                                          ]
[       [PLAYLIST]                         ]
[        Element                           ]
[                                          ]
```

**Recommended Elements:**
1. **Subscribe button** - Always include
2. **Best for viewer** - Algorithm-selected video
3. **Specific video** - Related content from same playlist
4. **Playlist** - Encourage continued watching

**Sleep Video Consideration:**
- Assume viewers may be asleep
- Don't use jarring end screens
- Consider removing auto-play recommendations
- Soft fade to black before end screen

---

### 7.2 Card Strategy (During Video)

**Card Timing for Long-Form Content:**

| Timestamp | Card Type | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 0:30-1:00 | Playlist | Anchor in longer session |
| 25% point | Video | Related shorter/longer version |
| 50% point | Video | Different visual style |
| 75% point | Playlist | Encourage series watching |
| 90% point | Channel | Subscribe reminder |

**Card Best Practices:**
- Maximum 5 cards per video
- Don't use cards in sleep content (disruptive)
- Test card performance in YouTube Analytics
- Use teaser text: "Prefer a shorter session?" or "Try our VR version"
